Betroot Hummus

Ever since I discovered this beetroot hummus recipe, it has been my favorite appetizer. I'm making it A-L-L the time. It always disappears minutes after I serve it! I highly recommend making a double batch if you're feeding more than 6 people.

For this recipe you will need:

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 2 cooked beetroots/li>
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • lemon juice

Directions

  1. Place the garlic, beetroot and chickpeas into a food processor. Mix for one minute until ground. Add olive oil until it becomes creamy.

  2. Add seasonings like salt and lemon juice if needed.
  3. Scrape and serve in a bowl.
