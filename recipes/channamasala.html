Channa Masala

Plump chickpeas bathed in a spicy and tangy tomato sauce. A healthy, cheap and quick winter meal

As one of the most popular dishes in the world, both in and out of India and Pakistan, channa masala or chana masala, chole masala, or chholay, depending on where you're from chickpeas cooked in a spicy and tangy tomato-based sauce. Of course, if you ask two people what constitutes an authentic version of the dish, you may get two opposite answers.

For this recipe you will need:

  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • ginger
  • one large onion
  • chickpeas
  • canned tomatoes
  • Masala spice

Directions

  1. Sautee garlic, giger and onion.
  2. Add the masala spice and mix until well browned.
  3. Add the chickpeas and canned tomato, then bring to a simmer.
  4. Serve with rice and top with coriander.
